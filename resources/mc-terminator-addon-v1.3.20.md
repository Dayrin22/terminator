          <p>
            <strong>Introducing Terminator to your Minecraft world! </strong
            >This is a powerful mob that has the ability to
            <mark class="marker-blue"
              >follow entities, destroy blocks and build blocks</mark
            >
            when the terminator thinks it's necessary,
            <mark class="marker-blue">pick up melee and ranged weapons</mark>
            such as swords and bows to attack entities,
            <mark class="marker-blue">and wear armors</mark> such as chestplates
            to protect itself from being damaged.
          </p>

            <h3><strong>Terminator Combat</strong></h3>
            <p>
              The <strong>Terminator Combat</strong> is a major update released
              for<strong> Terminator Combat Update</strong> (add-on version
              1.3).
            </p>
            <p>
              In order for the terminator to use different kinds of combat, they
              need to pick up the weapon in order to switch combat mechanic.
            </p>

            <p>&nbsp;</p>
            <h4><strong>Melee Attack</strong></h4>
            <p><strong>Melee attack is activated when</strong></p>
            <ul>
              <li>The terminator is spawned</li>
              <li>
                The terminator is holding a melee weapon (trident is excluded
                from this category)
              </li>
            </ul>
            <p><strong>Features</strong></p>
            <ul>
              <li>Movement speed multiplier of terminator is 130%</li>
              <li>It does 1 attack damage</li>
            </ul>
            <p>&nbsp;</p>
            <h4><strong>Bow</strong></h4>
            <p>Bow combat is activated when the terminator is holding a bow</p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                Terminator will wait 1 to 3 seconds after an attack before
                launching another
              </li>
              <li>
                Terminator shoots normal arrows (It will not shoot different
                effects of arrows even if terminator receives it.)
              </li>
            </ul>
            <p>&nbsp;</p>
            <h4><strong>Crossbow</strong></h4>
            <p>
              Crossbow combat is activated when the terminator is holding a
              crossbow
            </p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                Terminator will wait 1 second after an attack before launching
                another
              </li>
              <li>
                Terminator shoots normal arrows (It will not shoot different
                effects of arrows even if terminator receives it.)
              </li>
              <li>Movement speed multiplier of terminator is 100%</li>
            </ul>
            <p>&nbsp;</p>
            <h4><strong>Trident</strong></h4>
            <p>
              Trident combat is activated when the terminator is holding a
              trident
            </p>
            <ul>
              <li>
                Terminator will switch to melee combat if the target is 3 blocks
                or less away from the terminator
              </li>
              <li>
                Terminator will switch to ranged combat if the target is 4
                blocks or more away from the terminator
              </li>
            </ul>
            <p>&nbsp;</p>
            <h4><strong>Snowball</strong></h4>
            <p>
              Snowball combat activates when the terminator holds a snowball
            </p>
